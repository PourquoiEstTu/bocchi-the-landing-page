Welcome to kessoku band's (unofficial) website!

kessoku band is a 4-piece rock band formed in Shimo-kitazawa, Tokyo, Japan. The band is currently signed to Stray Beat label and has released two albums, Self-titled and Into the Light.

Meet the Band Members!

Gotoh "Bocchi" Hitori. The lead guitarist. Has extreme social anxiety.

Ijichi Nijika. The drummer and leader. Keeps the band together with her positivity. Is the paragon of human virtue.

Yamada Ryo. The bassist, composer, and arranger. Is constantly buying music equipment and is often penniless as a result. Can usually be seen eating weeds or borrowing money from Kita or Bocchi for food.

Kita Ikuyo. The vocalist and rhythm guitar. Is an extreme extrovert. Has a quite a few followers on Instagram and is the Minister of Social Media.
